FORM-SPECIFIC EMAIL AUTOMATIONS
-----------------------------------

1) FREE CONSULTATION FORM

Trigger:
When user submits the consultation form

Subject:
Your consultation request has been received

Body intent:
- Thank the user for submitting the consultation form
- Confirm that the request has been received
- Tell them our team will review the details and get back to them shortly
- Make it sound warm and reassuring
- Encourage them to keep an eye on their inbox

Suggested message direction:
"Thank you for booking a consultation with Universitio. We have received your request and our team will review your details shortly. Please keep an eye on your email, as we will contact you with the next steps."

2) ASSESSMENT FORM

Trigger:
When user submits the assessment form

SPECIAL REQUIREMENT:
This email must include the assessment result and next-step recommendation based on the submitted result.

Requirements:
- Include a summary of the assessment outcome
- If the system already calculates an assessment result, include that result in the email
- If there are categories such as strong / moderate / needs improvement / eligible / not yet ready, reflect that in the email
- Tailor the message based on the result
- End with a strong recommendation to book a consultation so we can help properly

Subject:
Your assessment result from Universitio

Body intent:
- Confirm assessment received
- Show the result clearly
- Give a short explanation of what the result means
- Suggest the next step
- Strong CTA to book a consultation

Example logic:
- If result is strong / high chance:
  congratulate briefly and encourage consultation to move forward correctly
- If result is moderate:
  explain that there is potential but details matter, and suggest consultation
- If result is weak / incomplete:
  explain that there may be issues to improve, and suggest consultation to strengthen the case

Suggested ending direction:
"Based on your assessment, we strongly recommend booking a consultation so we can review your case properly and guide you toward the best next step."

Also include CTA link if available:
- /free-consultation
