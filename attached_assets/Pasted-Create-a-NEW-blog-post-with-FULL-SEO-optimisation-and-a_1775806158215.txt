<p>Tuition fees vary depending on the university, course, and level of study.</p>

<p>Usually range from £12,000 to £25,000 per year, with MBA and specialised courses costing more.</p>

<p>The cost of living depends heavily on location.</p>

<h3>London</h3>
<p>£1,200 to £1,500 per month</p>

<p>£800 to £1,200 per month</p>
