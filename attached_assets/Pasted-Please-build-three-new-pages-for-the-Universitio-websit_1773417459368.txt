==================================================

Create a dedicated page for organisations, counsellors, agencies, introducers, and education partners who want to collaborate with Universitio.

Page goal:
To collect professional partnership enquiries in a clean, credible, and conversion-focused way.

Suggested route:
- /partners

Page structure:
- Hero/intro section
- Short explanatory content about partnership opportunities
- Modern professional form
- Trust-building layout
- Strong submit CTA

Hero section:
Create a clean and professional hero section with:
- headline
- short supporting paragraph
- clear call to action to complete the form

Suggested messaging direction:
- “Become a Universitio Partner”
- “Work with us to support international students applying to universities worldwide.”
- “If you are an education agency, counsellor, introducer, or student recruitment partner, we would be glad to learn more about your organisation.”

Form fields for Partner page:
- Full Name (required)
- Position / Job Title (required)
- Organisation Name (required)
- Email Address (required)
- Contact Number (required, with international country code selector if possible)
- Organisation Website (optional but recommended)
- Short Description of Services (required, textarea)
- Which student nationalities do you mainly work with? (required)
  - make this a country multi-select list
  - allow selecting multiple nationalities
- Which country is your organisation based in? (required)
  - single country dropdown
- Which study destinations are you interested in working with for student applications? (required)
  - multi-select options such as:
    - United Kingdom
    - United States
    - Canada
    - Germany
    - Netherlands
    - Australia
    - Europe (general)
- Additional Notes / Comments (optional, textarea)

UX notes:
- Group the fields clearly into sections if useful
- Keep the form clean and not overwhelming
- Use a polished layout with strong spacing
- Make it feel credible and business-oriented

Submit behaviour:
After successful submission, show a polished success message or modal:
“Thank you for your interest in partnering with Universitio. Your enquiry has been received, and our team will review your information and contact you shortly.”

2. STUDENT REFERRAL PROGRAMME PAGE
==================================================

Create a dedicated page for students who want to refer other students to Universitio.

Suggested route:
- /student-referral

Page goal:
To collect referral programme applications in a friendly, trustworthy, and clear way.

Important messaging:
This programme is for students who want to refer potential applicants.
Commission/reward will only be considered after the referred student successfully enrols.
The page should be clear, transparent, and not feel spammy.

Page structure:
- Hero/intro section
- Short explanation of how the programme works
- Modern referral form
- Submit CTA
- Trustworthy messaging

Hero section:
Create a welcoming but professional hero section with:
- headline
- short paragraph
- simple explanation of the programme

Suggested direction:
- “Join the Universitio Student Referral Programme”
- “Refer students who are planning to study abroad and apply through Universitio.”
- “Applications are reviewed individually, and accepted applicants will be contacted by email.”

Important note to show clearly on the page:
After submitting the form, the applicant may be accepted or rejected, and they will be informed by email.

Form fields for Student Referral page:
- Referrer Full Name (required)
- Email Address (required)
- Date of Birth (required)
- Name of the University where they currently study (required)
- Which student nationalities are you planning to refer? (required)
  - country multi-select list
  - allow selecting as many as needed
- Which destination countries do you want to refer students for? (required)
  - multi-select options such as:
    - United Kingdom
    - United States
    - Canada
    - Germany
    - Netherlands
    - Australia
    - Europe (general)
- Additional Notes / Comments (optional, textarea)

UX notes:
- Make the form friendly, clear, and easy to complete
- Keep it minimal, modern, and mobile-friendly
- Use clean field grouping and spacing
- Make the programme feel legitimate and transparent

Submit behaviour:
After successful submission, show a polished success message or modal:
“Thank you. Your referral programme application has been received successfully. Our team will review your submission, and you will be informed by email if your application is accepted or not.”
