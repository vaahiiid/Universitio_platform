# Universitio Platform

Universitio is a digital platform designed to support international students throughout the entire journey of studying abroad, from initial decision making to application and beyond.

The platform combines structured guidance, conversion workflows, and AI powered support to simplify a highly fragmented and high risk process.

---

## AskiMate AI

AskiMate is the core intelligence layer inside Universitio.

It is not a generic chatbot. It is a context aware decision support system designed to:

- Understand user intent
- Retrieve relevant knowledge from a structured knowledge base
- Evaluate confidence and risk levels
- Provide safe, contextual, and actionable guidance
- Escalate complex or high risk cases to human mentors

This hybrid AI + human system is designed to build trust and reduce misinformation in international education decisions.

---

## Problem

International students often rely on fragmented, inconsistent, and sometimes misleading information when applying to universities.

This leads to:

- Poor application decisions
- Visa rejections
- Financial risks
- Lack of trust in existing agents and platforms

---

## Solution

Universitio provides:

- Structured application guidance
- Smart assessment tools
- AI driven support through AskiMate
- Human mentor escalation for critical cases
- Conversion focused workflows (consultation, referrals, onboarding)
